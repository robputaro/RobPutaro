Site care

The website should not become another thing your team has to manage.

A custom site is most useful when someone keeps it accurate, connected, and easy to improve. The care layer turns the site from a one-time launch into a maintained asset.

Ongoing support

Small updates, cleaner upkeep, fewer abandoned pages.

Basic Care

For clinics that want the site hosted, watched, and updated without asking internal staff to handle technical details.

  • Hosting management
  • Small text, image, and link updates
  • Monthly link and form check
  • Basic technical troubleshooting
  • Minor SEO/meta adjustments

Care Plus

For clinics that want ongoing improvement, not just maintenance. Best when the site supports campaigns, service pushes, or seasonal priorities.

  • Everything in Basic Care
  • Up to 2 hours of monthly edits
  • Quarterly content and SEO recommendations
  • New section planning
  • Priority support
Scope control

Care is not unlimited redesign work hiding in a monthly fee.

Minor updates are included. New pages, major design changes, new integrations, advanced SEO projects, ads, and large content builds are scoped separately.
